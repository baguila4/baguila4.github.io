Website Evaluations

Welcome to my Website Evaluations Page! Here you will see two websites evaluated based of the CRAP Principles.

Nike

Nike's website showcases their sports apparel and equipment with a sleek, modern design focused on user experience and ease of shopping.

  • Contrast: High contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of branding elements like fonts and colors.
  • Alignment: Content is organized in a clean, grid-based layout.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together, simplifying user decisions.

Nike employs CRAP principles effectively to provide an engaging user interface.

LingsCars

LingsCars offers car leasing and rental services through a chaotic and visually overwhelming website design.

  • Contrast: Very poor, with text blending into odd backgrounds.
  • Repetition: Lacks consistency in fonts, colors, and styles.
  • Alignment: Random placement of elements.
  • Proximity: Fails to group related content logically, making naviagtion complicated.

LingsCars does not adhere to CRAP principles, resulting in a confusing user interface.
